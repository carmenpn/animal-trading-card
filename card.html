Ragdoll Kitten

The name "Ragdoll" comes from the fact that they go limp when picked up.

  • Average weight: 10-15 pounds
  • Average lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Temperament: Mellow
  • Habitat: Loving family

Ragdoll cats are particularly known for their blue eyes and placid temperament. Even though they are docile, they like to play. Ragdoll cats are very affectionate and enjoy human interaction.
